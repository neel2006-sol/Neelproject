About Us

Welcome to RULZZZ Apparel – where style meets the street! We are a trendy urban clothing brand designed for those who are bold, unique, and love to make a statement. Our mission is to bring high-quality, fashionable, and comfortable clothing to everyone who embraces streetwear culture. Whether you're looking to upgrade your wardrobe or make a statement with our limited-edition collections, we’ve got you covered.

At RULZZZ, we believe that fashion is not just about clothes; it’s about confidence, attitude, and expressing yourself. Join the movement and wear your style with pride.

The Founders

RULZZZ was founded by three passionate individuals who share a love for street culture and fashion. Together, they combined their talents and vision to create a brand that celebrates individuality and creativity. Here's a little more about the founders:

Solapuri Neel

Co-Founder & Operations Manager

Neel is the operations mastermind behind RULZZZ. With a keen eye for logistics and a deep understanding of the streetwear industry, he ensures the brand delivers top-quality products.

Khajja Varun

Co-Founder & Marketing Director

Varun is the driving force behind RULZZZ’s marketing strategies. His campaigns and social media presence have helped propel RULZZZ into the spotlight.

Dedaniya Mihir

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Mihir is the visionary behind RULZZZ's creative direction. With a background in graphic design and street art, Mihir brings the bold and unique styles that define the brand.

Together, John, Jane, and Michael have transformed RULZZZ into more than just a brand – it's a lifestyle.
